    • Must visit Destinations

    From historical cities to natural splendours, come see the best of India.

    Dalhousie

    Perched atop a rocky ridge, with snow-clad Dhauladhar mountains and rolling emerald valleys adding to its natural beauty. Dalhousie, in Himachal Pradesh, is a quaint hill town perfect for a relaxing holiday.

    Leh

    A surreal landscape surrounded by the sky-piercing peaks of the snow-capped Himalayan, Zanskar and Karakoram ranges.

    Shillong

    Colonial-era charm, a landscape of rolling green and pristine mountains come together to bring to life the city of Shillong.

    The Statue of Unity

    The 182-metre (600 feet approx.) statue is dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, the architect of independent India.

    Wayanad

    A coffer of coffee, cardamom and tea plantations, which envelope the land with lush emerald greenery and fill the air with a heady aroma, Wayanad, in Kerala.

    What to do in an emergency?

    In case of an emergency, dial:

    • 1- 100 or 112 for POLICE
    • 2- 101 for FIRE
    • 3- 102 for AMBULANCE
