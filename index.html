    • Must visit Destinations

    From historical cities to natural splendours, come see the best of India.

    Dalhousie

    Perched atop a rocky ridge, with snow-clad Dhauladhar mountains and rolling emerald valleys adding to its natural beauty. Dalhousie, in Himachal Pradesh, is a quaint hill town perfect for a relaxing holiday.

    Leh

    A surreal landscape surrounded by the sky-piercing peaks of the snow-capped Himalayan, Zanskar and Karakoram ranges.

    Shillong

    Colonial-era charm, a landscape of rolling green and pristine mountains come together to bring to life the city of Shillong.

    Kevadia

    The Statue of Unity, an 182-meter (approx.) statue, is dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, the architect of independent India.

    Wayanad

    A coffer of coffee, cardamom and tea plantations, which envelope the land with lush emerald greenery and fill the air with a heady aroma, Wayanad, in Kerala.

    Health and safety travel tips

    Travel Advice

    The traveler within us is always planning that next trip. Across India, hotels, restaurants and tourist attractions are open and ready to be enjoyed. Of course, the best way to make the most of any trip is to stay up to speed on the latest health and travel advice, and to take practical precautions. So, whether you’re planning a luxury getaway or a cross-country road trip, here are some tips to make your Indian adventure worry- and stress-free.

    Stay Informed

    Following government guidelines can provide the latest advice about travel to and within India.

    Safety tips for travelers in India

    • Be mindful of your day bag and purse at all times
    • Research the places you'll be visiting to learn about the local culture and customs – India is extremely diverse and each place you go can be vastly different from the last!
    • Avoid any areas experiencing political or civil unrest
